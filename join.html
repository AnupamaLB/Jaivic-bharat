Donation Policy & Transparency (Jaivic Foundation)

  • Purpose: Donations support nature-centric, community-led work across health, habitat, livelihoods, culture, and ecological regeneration.
  • Where it goes: Contributions are received through Jaivic Foundation and routed to verified initiatives, including partner NGOs and foundations aligned with the mission.
  • Cause preference: If you specify a cause, we make best efforts to route it accordingly. If exact routing isn't feasible, it supports the closest aligned initiative or where it's needed most.
  • Receipt: You will receive an acknowledgement/receipt on your provided email.
  • Use of funds: Program delivery, community activities, materials/tools, training, field operations, and necessary administration.
  • Reporting: Periodic updates on outcomes through official channels and, where possible, direct donor updates.
  • Privacy: Donor data is used only for receipts, donation communication, and updates; we do not sell donor data.
