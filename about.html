About Jaivic Bharat

Nature-Aligned Living, Led by Citizens

Our Vision

An India where every community thrives in harmony with nature, leaving zero negative impact on the environment and handing over a better Earth to future generations.

Better Earth Harmony

Our Mission

To regenerate India through nature-aligned living systems across four pillars.

Four Pillars Framework

Our Focus

We concentrate our efforts on four interconnected pillars that form the foundation of nature-aligned living:

Health

Holistic well-being through nature-aligned health practices

Habitat

Sustainable living spaces in harmony with natural ecosystems

Livelihoods

Regenerative economies rooted in local resources and skills

Culture

Reviving traditions that honor our connection to nature

Our Story

Jaivic Bharat began in 2015 when farmers, ecologists, and community leaders in Northern India united to address declining soil health and vanishing traditional knowledge.

What started as organic farming awareness meetings and campaigns with intellectuals and farmer leaders has grown into a nationwide movement.

The name "Jaivic" comes from Sanskrit, meaning "life-giving" – representing our commitment to regenerative systems.
